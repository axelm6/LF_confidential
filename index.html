🌿 The LiveFresh Model

From farm to you — nothing in between.

Most produce travels through warehouses, distributors and cold storage before it reaches you. We cut the chain down to its freshest possible path.

🌾1

The Farm

We partner directly with growers and pick at peak ripeness — not weeks early for shipping.

📦2

The LiveFresh Hub

Sorted, quality-checked and packed the same day. No long-term cold storage, no freezers.

🚚3

Direct Delivery

Routed straight to your door or stocked at our neighborhood stores within the day.

🏡4

Your Table

Fresh fruits, vegetables and proteins arrive at their best — affordable, every time.
